About

Izabela Tolentino

Technical Game Designer

izabela.tolentino@gmail.com

I am a technical game designer who loves to convey stories through games. Whether it’s through how the main character deals with obstacles, what the main objectives are, or even what kinds of rewards the player may get, I enjoy designing and playing games that players can both enjoy and learn something from.

I have 5 years of experience in AAA live-service development from my time at Bungie on Destiny 2 from Witch Queen (2022) all the way to the game's final major update Monument of Triumph (2026). My extensive hours as a player and growing experience as a developer enables me to implement & iterate on various game elements given its goals, constraints, and feedback from others in a highly collaborative environment. I also have a B.S. in Math-Computer Science that helped lay a strong foundation for my technical skills and problem solving.

In addition to playing games, I love to draw, dance, take photos, watch anime, and browse through cute dog pictures during my free time.
